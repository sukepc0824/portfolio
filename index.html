Visit Site

I have produced an instructional video on Hackintosh, a method of installing macOS on windows. Introduces techniques and benchmarks related to bootloaders. The video received over 50,000 views within a week after it was posted.

UI Design/Web Development

Quick Searcher

Visit Site

Based on the concept of "one-shot search for what you want to find," I developed "Quick Searcher," a tool that allows you to search various search engines at once. I am conscious of the refinement of UI design and overall accessibility.

「探したいものに一発で」をコンセプトに，様々な検索エンジンで一度に検索できるツールQuick Searcherを開発しました。 UIデザインの洗練や全体のアクセシビリティを意識しています。
